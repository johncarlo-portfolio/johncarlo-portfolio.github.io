Hello!

Clare Dolotina is a freelance fashion model and fitness enthusiast based in Metro Manila, Philippines.

She started her modeling career back in college while she was studying at the University of Sto. Tomas.

Her passion for fitness began as she felt the need to have a healthier lifestyle. With the help of a strict diet and regular exercise, she achieved her goal in just a few months. From 148lbs, she now maintains her weight at 118lbs and enjoys her slim figure. Clare's weight-loss journey inspired the people around her to do the same thing -to have a healthy lifestyle!
